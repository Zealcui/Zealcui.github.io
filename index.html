I just obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Washington University in St Louis under the supervision of Prof. Yixin Chen. I received my B.E. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology at The University of Science and Technology of China in 2014. My research interests are deep learning, interpretable machine learning and security of machine learning. I'm joining Google in Nov. 2019.
